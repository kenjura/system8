# Nature

Nature measures your ability to recall lore about terrain, plants and animals, the weather, and natural cycles.

## How to Roll

**Nature = d20 + Intelligence modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)**

## Common Uses

### Identify Plants and Animals
Recognize species, know their properties, and understand their behavior.
- **DC 10**: Common plants and animals
- **DC 15**: Uncommon species or regional variations
- **DC 20**: Rare species or those from distant regions
- **DC 25+**: Legendary or magical creatures (in their natural aspects)

### Predict Weather
Anticipate changes in weather patterns over the next 24 hours.

### Navigate Wilderness
Find your way through natural environments without getting lost.

### Identify Terrain
Recognize the characteristics of different terrains and what dangers they might present.

### Know Natural Hazards
Identify poisonous plants, dangerous animals, or environmental hazards.

### Understand Ecosystems
Know how plants, animals, and environments interact and depend on each other.

## Proficiency Benefits
- You can identify whether plants are safe to eat.
- You can predict weather up to 3 days in advance.

## Expertise Benefits
- You automatically recognize common plants, animals, and terrain.
- You can find food and water in the wilderness without rolling.

## Mastery Benefits
- You can sense when something is unnatural about an environment.
- You gain advantage on survival checks in natural environments.

# Religion

Religion measures your ability to recall lore about deities, rites and prayers, religious hierarchies, holy symbols, and the practices of secret cults.

## How to Roll

**Religion = d20 + Intelligence modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)**

## Common Uses

### Recall Religious Lore
Remember information about gods, religious practices, or sacred texts.
- **DC 10**: Common knowledge about major religions
- **DC 15**: Details about religious ceremonies or minor deities
- **DC 20**: Obscure religious lore or ancient practices
- **DC 25+**: Secret or forbidden religious knowledge

### Identify Holy Symbols
Recognize the symbols, vestments, and iconography of various faiths.

### Understand Religious Practices
Know the proper rituals, prayers, and observances for various religions.

### Identify Undead and Fiends
Recognize different types of undead, fiends, and celestials, and know their weaknesses.

### Recognize Divine Magic
Identify divine spells and their sources.

### Know Religious History
Understand the history of religious conflicts, schisms, and reforms.

## Proficiency Benefits
- You can perform basic religious ceremonies for faiths you know.
- You can identify divine magic and its likely source.

## Expertise Benefits
- You know the weaknesses of undead and fiends you can identify.
- You can recognize when someone is lying about their religious affiliation.

## Mastery Benefits
- You automatically recall common religious knowledge.
- You can sense the presence of consecrated or desecrated ground.

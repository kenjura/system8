# Arcana

Arcana measures your ability to recall lore about spells, magic items, eldritch symbols, magical traditions, the planes of existence, and the inhabitants of those planes.

## How to Roll

**Arcana = d20 + Intelligence modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)**

## Common Uses

### Identify a Spell
Recognize a spell being cast or identify the lingering effects of a spell. The DC depends on the spell's rarity and your familiarity with the tradition.
- **DC 10**: A common cantrip or 1st-level spell
- **DC 15**: A moderately common spell (2nd-4th level)
- **DC 20**: A rare or higher-level spell (5th-7th level)
- **DC 25+**: An obscure or legendary spell (8th-9th level)

### Identify a Magic Item
Determine the properties of a magic item through study and experimentation (typically requires a short rest).

### Recall Magical Lore
Remember information about magical creatures, planar entities, arcane traditions, or magical phenomena.

### Detect Magical Auras
With concentration, sense the presence of magic in an area (though Detect Magic is more reliable).

## Proficiency Benefits
- You can attempt to identify spells as they are being cast.
- You can use Arcana to attempt counterspells or dispel effects.

## Expertise Benefits
- You can identify magic items without requiring a short rest.
- You gain insight into the weaknesses of magical creatures.

## Mastery Benefits
- You automatically recognize common spells and magic items.
- You can sense when powerful magic has been used in an area recently.

# Performance

Performance determines how well you can delight an audience with music, dance, acting, storytelling, or some other form of entertainment.

## How to Roll

**Performance = d20 + Charisma modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)**

## Common Uses

### Entertain an Audience
Perform for a crowd. The DC depends on the audience's expectations:
- **DC 10**: Entertain common folk with simple entertainment
- **DC 15**: Impress a discerning audience or perform competently
- **DC 20**: Wow a sophisticated audience or deliver a memorable performance
- **DC 25+**: Create a legendary performance that will be remembered for years

### Earn Money
Use your talents to earn coin during downtime. Better performances earn more money.

### Create a Distraction
Use your performance to draw attention away from something else.

### Inspire

### Disguise Through Acting
Assume a role convincingly, using acting skills to support a disguise.

### Play an Instrument
Requires proficiency with the specific instrument. This applies to singing as well.

## Proficiency Benefits
- You can play musical instruments and perform professionally.
- You can earn a modest living through performance during downtime.

## Expertise Benefits
- You can inspire allies, granting them advantage on their next ability check.
- Your performances can influence the mood of a crowd.

## Mastery Benefits
- Your performances can have magical effects (inspire courage, cause fear, etc.).
- You automatically succeed at entertaining common audiences.

# History

History measures your ability to recall lore about historical events, legendary people, ancient kingdoms, past disputes, recent wars, and lost civilizations.

## How to Roll

**History = d20 + Intelligence modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)**

## Common Uses

### Recall Historical Events
Remember significant events from the past. The DC depends on how obscure the information is:
- **DC 10**: Common knowledge (major wars, famous rulers)
- **DC 15**: Specialized knowledge (details of treaties, lesser nobles)
- **DC 20**: Obscure information (ancient dynasties, forgotten battles)
- **DC 25+**: Lost or secret history

### Identify Historical Artifacts
Recognize the origin, age, and significance of historical objects, ruins, or documents.

### Understand Cultural Context
Know the customs, traditions, and social structures of past civilizations.

### Research
Conduct effective research in libraries, archives, or other repositories of knowledge.

### Recognize Heraldry
Identify coats of arms, banners, and other symbols of lineage and allegiance.

## Proficiency Benefits
- You can read ancient or archaic forms of languages you know.
- You can estimate the age and origin of historical artifacts.

## Expertise Benefits
- You can recall detailed information about historical figures and events.
- Your research takes half the normal time.

## Mastery Benefits
- You automatically recall common historical facts without rolling.
- You can make educated guesses about historical events you haven't studied.

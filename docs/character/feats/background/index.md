Background feats represent talents, training, and cultural knowledge a character has acquired from their upbringing, homeland, or life experiences.


# Talents

- [Acting](acting): Charisma bonus to untrained skills and advantage on Deception to play a role.
- [Musical Talent](musical-talent): Proficiency in Perform and access to Bardic Inspiration.


# Culture

- [Orcish Battle-rage](orcish-battle-rage): Encounter ability granting bonus melee damage and DR.
- [Gift of the Eldar](gift-of-the-eldar): Call upon nature for minor environmental favors.
- [Cast-Iron Stomach](cast-iron-stomach): Resistance to disease and poison from Earthen heritage.
- [Elf Step](elf-step): Move silently without tracks and ignore natural difficult terrain.
- [Northern Born](northern-born): Resist cold 50% and ignore natural cold penalties.
- [Shadowmeld](shadowmeld): Become invisible while immobile in natural foliage.

*Skill Feat*

# Animal Handler

You can calm a hostile beast or gain a mount's trust with a few moments of interaction.

You can train animals in half the normal time.

While mounted, you gain advantage on checks to control your mount, and your mount gains +2 to AC against attacks targeting it.

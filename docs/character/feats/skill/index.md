Skill feats are traits available to all PCs that reflect the expertise and training they have acquired in various skills. 


# Strength Skill Feats

- [Athletic](athletic): Enhanced jumping, swimming, climbing, and sprinting capabilities.



# Dexterity Skill Feats

- [Acrobatic](acrobatic): Tumble through enemies, evade area effects, and reduce falling damage.
- [Sleight of Hand](sleight-of-hand): Quick hands for drawing weapons, picking pockets, and disabling traps.
- [Stealthy](stealthy): Hide while observed and move at full speed without breaking stealth.



# Intelligence Skill Feats

- [Arcane Lore](arcane-lore): Identify spells, magical creatures, and arcane phenomena.
- [Historical Lore](historical-lore): Recall historical events, identify artifacts, and appraise antiques.
- [Investigative](investigative): Search quickly, find hidden clues, and deduce events from evidence.
- [Natural Lore](natural-lore): Identify flora, fauna, terrain, and predict natural phenomena.
- [Religious Lore](religious-lore): Identify holy symbols, religious rites, and recall undead weaknesses.



# Wisdom Skill Feats

- [Animal Handler](animal-handler): Calm beasts, train animals quickly, and excel at mounted combat.
- [Insightful](insightful): Detect lies, read emotions, and discern NPC motivations.
- [Medical Training](medical-training): Stabilize dying creatures quickly and enhance healing effects.
- [Perceptive](perceptive): Cannot be surprised, notice hidden things, and lip-read conversations.
- [Survival Training](survival-training): Track creatures, forage for food, and navigate any terrain.



# Charisma Skill Feats

- [Deceptive](deceptive): Lie convincingly, maintain false identities, and feint in combat.
- [Intimidating](intimidating): Demoralize enemies as a swift action, penalizing their attacks.
- [Performer](performer): Captivate audiences, create distractions, and impersonate voices.
- [Persuasive](persuasive): Negotiate favorable terms and shift NPC attitudes through diplomacy.

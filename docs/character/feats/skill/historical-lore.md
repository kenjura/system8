*Skill Feat*

# Historical Lore

You can recall significant events, legendary figures, ancient kingdoms, and past disputes.

When encountering ruins, artifacts, or heraldry, you can attempt to identify their origin and significance.

You gain advantage on checks to appraise antiques and historical artifacts.

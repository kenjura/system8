Ancestry feats allow a character to gain abilities or traits related to their ancestry, reflecting supernatural bloodlines, magical heritage, or otherworldly origins.


# Aberration

- [Psychic Symbiote](psychic-symbiote): Host an alien mind that shares concentration and negates mental saves.


# Dragon

- [Rage of the Dragon](rage-of-the-dragon): Channel draconic fury through rage, breath weapons, and transformation.


# Sylvan

- [Aquatic Nature](aquatic-nature): Transform into a water creature with swim speed and water breathing.
- [Glamour](glamour): Magically enhance your appearance for advantage on Charisma checks.
- [Siren Song](siren-song): Enchanting voice grants Perform advantage and one Bardic Music feat.
- [Furry](furry): Stable human-animal hybrid form with bestial traits.
- [Plantoid](plantoid): Photosynthetic being that gains sustenance from sunlight.
- [Werefolk](werefolk): Shift between human and animal forms with Call of the Wild.
- [Underwater Native](underwater-native): Breathe underwater with swim speed, but require daily water time.
- [Fey](fey): Gain fey nature with iron vulnerability and binding by words or gifts.


# Tiefling

- [Born in Flames](born-in-flames): Gain Resist 50% fire damage.
- [Firestarter](firestarter): Shed flames at will, adding fire damage to unarmed attacks.
- [Infernal Chains](infernal-chains): Sprout spiked chains usable as reach weapons.

## Rank 2

- [Demon Wings](demon-wings): Gain slowfall and glide at will with batlike wings.

## Rank 3

- [Blood Like Fire](blood-like-fire): Gain Immunity to Fire. Requires Born in Flames.
- [Wings of the Fallen](wings-of-the-fallen): Gain fly speed equal to walking speed. Requires Demon Wings.


# Shadow

- [Cloaked in Shadow](cloaked-in-shadow): Gain AC bonus when attackers have concealment against you.
- [Withering Touch](withering-touch): Touch attack drains life, dealing necrotic and Con damage.

## Rank 2

- [Devilsight](devilsight): Gain Darkvision.


# Netherkin

- [Kiss of Death](kiss-of-death): Necrotic touch attack that can heal you or boost rolls.
- [Improved Kiss of Death](improved-kiss-of-death): Enhanced Kiss deals heavy damage plus Cha damage.

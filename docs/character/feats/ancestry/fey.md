*Ancestry Feat*

# Fey

You have fey nature with the following traits:

- **Iron Vulnerability:** You are vulnerable to cold iron weapons and effects.
- **Ward Susceptibility:** You can be warded by effects that work on outsiders.
- **Binding:** You can be bound by your words or by receiving gifts.

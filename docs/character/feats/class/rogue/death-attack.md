*Rogue Maneuver (Assassin)*

# Death Attack

**At-will, Standard Action**

You prepare a death attack against the subject, granting +100% damage to your next Sneak Attack against them. This can stack up to +300%. All bonus damage must be used within the encounter, or it is lost.

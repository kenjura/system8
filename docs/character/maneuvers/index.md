# Maneuvers

Maneuvers are combat techniques that characters can use in battle. They are divided into categories based on availability.

## [Basic Maneuvers](./basic/)

Available to all characters regardless of class. Includes fundamental techniques like blocking, dodging, parrying, and grappling.

## [Fighter Maneuvers](./fighter/)

Combat techniques exclusive to the Fighter class, organized by combat style:
- **Assault** - Aggressive offensive maneuvers
- **Protection** - Defensive techniques to protect allies
- **Ranged** - Archery and ranged combat techniques
- **Warlord** - Leadership abilities that buff allies
- **Skirmisher** - Mobile hit-and-run tactics
- **Group Fighting** - Techniques for fighting multiple enemies

## [Rogue Maneuvers](./rogue/)

Sneaky techniques exclusive to the Rogue class, organized by specialization:
- **Regular Rogue Stuff** - Core rogue tricks
- **Assassin** - Deadly killing techniques
- **Shadowdancer** - Shadow magic and teleportation
- **Spellthief** - Stealing magical abilities

*Rogue Maneuver (Assassin)*

# Void Strike

**1 soul shard, Swift**

You whisper words of Shadow magic, imbuing your melee weapon with the power of the Void. Your next successful melee attack also dispels one magical effect on the subject, if the attack roll exceeds that effect's DC.

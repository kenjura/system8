*Rogue Maneuver*

# Feint

**At-will, Swift, Melee range**

Roll Deception vs Perception. On success, the enemy uses their reaction to avoid the fictitious attack. (Against a PC, roll both rolls secretly, and tell the PC if they are hit; they can still decide if they want to use their reaction).

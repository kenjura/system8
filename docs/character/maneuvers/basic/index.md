# Basic Maneuvers

Basic maneuvers are combat techniques available to all characters regardless of class.

## Offensive Maneuvers

- [Charge](./charge): Rush an enemy in a straight line to attack with advantage.
- [Disarm](./disarm): Force an enemy to drop their weapon.
- [Grapple](./grapple): Grab and restrain an enemy in close combat.
- [Shove](./shove): Push an enemy away from you.
- [Trip](./trip): Knock an enemy prone.

## Defensive Maneuvers

- [Block](./block): Use a shield to absorb and reduce incoming damage.
- [Dodge](./dodge): Avoid an incoming attack by moving out of the way.
- [Parry](./parry): Deflect an incoming melee attack with your weapon.

## Ranged Maneuvers

- [Aim](./aim): Gain advantage on your next ranged attack against a chosen target.

## Stealth Maneuvers

- [Hide](./hide): Become invisible to enemies who fail to perceive you.
- [Move Silently](./move-silently): Stay hidden while moving at reduced speed.

## Magic Maneuvers

- [Counterspell](./counterspell): Counter an opponent's spell as they cast it.
- [Dispel](./dispel): Use a spell to undo an enemy's active magical effect.

---
class: Thaumaturgist
name: Doom
school: Curse
rank: 3
type: Debilitation
brief: Curse an enemy to wither away, harm themselves, or fail at all endeavors
---

At-will, std, ranged, 3 vitae; you lay a curse upon the subject. Choose one:
- Doom of the Body: each round, the subject's max HP lowers by 25%.
- Doom of the Double-Edged Sword: each time the subject inflicts damage, they inflict equal unblockable damage to a randomly chosen ally or themself.
- Doom of Failure: subject suffers -1 to all rolls. On each turn start, this penalty doubles.

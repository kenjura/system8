*Class Feat*

# Smite

At-will, swift; you invoke your divine oath to oppose evil and wickedness. The next melee attack you land on a target who is an undead, fiend, or practitioner of necromancy or infernal magic, inflicts 50% base weapon damage as bonus radiant damage.

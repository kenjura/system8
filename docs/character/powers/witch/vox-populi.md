---
class: Thaumaturgist
name: Vox Populi
school: Voice
rank: 3
type: Control
brief: Control what creatures may say
---

At-will, std, emanation 10, 3 Vitae; you create a zone where creatures must speak according to your will. For instance, you might forbid lies, compel everyone to speak their secrets, or simply prevent any speech at all. Creatures that fail a Cha save when entering or starting their turn in the area are affected until they leave the area.

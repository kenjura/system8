---
class: Thaumaturgist
name: Bloodborne
school: Blood
rank: 2
type: Assault, Debilitation
brief: Infect a target with a bloodborne pathogen that hinders them in various ways
---

At-will, std, touch; you infect subject with a bloodborne pathogen. Choose one of the following effects:
- Hemophilia: slashing or piercing damage causes ongoing medium internal damage (Con ends).
- Sickle-cell: subject suffers debilitating pain, causing [wracked](../conditions/wracked.md) (Con ends).
- Anemia: subject suffers disadvantage to all Str, Dex, and Con checks and saves, and has half movement rate (Con ends).


Talents:
- Enhanced Pathogen: you can choose two of the above effects when you cast this power.
- Virulent Strain: when you infect a target, you can also cause them to infect one additional target within 5 feet of them. The new target must make a Con save against your spell DC or suffer the same effects.
- Persistent Infection: the effects last until cured by a remove disease spell or similar magic, rather than ending on a successful save.

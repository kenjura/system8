---
class: Witch
name: Arointment
school: Antimagic
rank: 4
type: Shaping
brief: Create an area where certain magic cannot function
---

At-will, swift, concentration, circle 1/rank; you create an invisible field which suppresses certain types of magic within its area. Choose any single school of magic when you create the arointment; spells and magical effects from that school cannot function within the area.

At rank 5, you can choose two schools of magic to suppress.


# Talents
- [Unfair Advantage](#): You can ignore the suppression field of an arointment you created.

---
class: Witch
name: Seven Eyes
school: Eyes
rank: 4
type: Assault,, Control, Debilitation, Enhancement
brief: You are surrounded by seven floating eyes that can see in all directions and manifest various powers
---

At-will, swift, 4 Vitae; you manifest seven spectral eyes that orbit around you, each capable of independent action. While active, you gain 360 degree vision, you see invisible and ethereal creatures, and you can perceive magical auras as per the Mystic Eyes power.

- Eye of Dread: recharge 33%, free, ranged; subject is frightened (Wis ends).
- Eye of Charm: recharge 33%, free, ranged; subject is charmed (Wis ends).
- Eye of Antimagic: recharge 33%, reaction to a spell being cast; the spell is nullified as it is cast using antimagic.
- Eye of Blinking: recharge 33%, reaction; the eye winks, and you (and your eyes) teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
- Eye of Entropy: recharge 33%, free, ranged; target takes heavy disintegration damage (Con half).
- Eye of Petrification: recharge 33%, free, ranged; target turns to stone (Con ends).
- Eye of Healing: recharge 33%, free, ranged; heal an ally for heavy damage.

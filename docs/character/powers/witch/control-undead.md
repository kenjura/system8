---
class: Thaumaturgist
name: Control Undead
school: Undeath
rank: 0
type: Control
brief: Control a mindless undead creature
---

At-will, std, ranged; subject mindless undead creature is under your control (Cha ends). If the undead is under the domination of someone else, you must beat their control DC, and they are allowed saves to regain control. At rank 2, you can affect intelligent undead as well.

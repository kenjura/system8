class: Psion
name: Wholeness of Body
school: Psychometabolism
rank: 1
type: Enhancement
brief: Heal yourself or another
---

At-will, swift; you heal yourself for up to 25% of your [Reserve Hit Points](../../basics/reserve-hit-points). This diminishes your reserve.

At-will, std, touch; you heal subject for up to 25% of their [Reserve Hit Points](../../basics/reserve-hit-points). This diminishes their reserve.

At rank 2, the maximum increases to 50%. At rank 3, it becomes 100%.

Talent ideas:
- Area healing
- Something about poison/disease/etc

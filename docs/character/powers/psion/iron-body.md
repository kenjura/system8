---
class: Psion
name: Iron Body
school: Biometabolism
rank: 1
type: Enhancement
brief: Harden your body to empower unarmed attacks, resist damage, repel poison, etc.
---

At-will, swift, concentration; you modify your body, focusing energy into parts of it to increase hardness and momentum.

While active, you gain the following benefits:
- Your unarmed strikes inflict heavy bludgeoning damage (or you can dual-wield medium damage)
- You gain a natural armor bonus equal to your proficiency plus Charisma.
- When rolling a saving throw against a poison, disease, or any effect that attempts to modify the constitution of your body (such as petrification or polymorphing), you may use your Psionics roll in place of the saving throw, if greater.

By expending Focus, you can achieve briefly achieve a state of bodily perfection. For one round per rank, you gain the following benefits:
- All of the normal benefits listed above
- Resist 50% to all physical damage
- Immune to poison and disease; existing effects are purged

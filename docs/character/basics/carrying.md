Carrying capacity is based primarily on Strength.


Which objects or creatures can be thrown, wielded, or lifted based on Strength, for a medium creature:

Str mod  | Max throw/wield | Max carry | Max lift
---------|-----------|-----------|-----------
+0 to +3 | Tiny      | Small     | Medium
+4 to +7 | Small     | Medium    | Large
+10 to +13 | Medium    | Large     | Huge
+14 to +17 | Large     | Huge      | Gargantuan
+18 to +21 | Huge      | Gargantuan| Colossal

# Actions in Combat

This page lists the various actions and maneuvers available during combat.

## Standard Actions

- [Attack](./actions/attack): Make a melee or ranged attack against a target.
- [Dash](./actions/dash): Move up to your speed as an additional move action.
- [Disengage](./actions/disengage): Move without provoking attacks of opportunity.
- [Aim](./actions/aim): Gain advantage on your next ranged attack against a chosen target.
- [Charge](./actions/charge): Rush an enemy in a straight line to attack with advantage.
- [Disarm](./actions/disarm): Force an enemy to drop their weapon.
- [Grapple](./actions/grapple): Grab and restrain an enemy in close combat.
- [Shove](./actions/shove): Push an enemy away from you.
- [Trip](./actions/trip): Knock an enemy prone.
- [Counterspell](./actions/counterspell): Counter an opponent's spell as they cast it.
- [Dispel](./actions/dispel): Use a spell to undo an enemy's active magical effect.

## Swift Actions

- [Hide](./actions/hide): Become invisible to enemies who fail to perceive you.
- [Move Silently](./actions/move-silently): Stay hidden while moving at reduced speed.

## Reactions

- [Dodge](./actions/dodge): Avoid an incoming attack by moving out of the way.
- [Parry](./actions/parry): Deflect an incoming melee attack with your weapon.
- [Block](./actions/block): Use a shield to absorb and reduce incoming damage.

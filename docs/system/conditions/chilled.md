# Chilled

A condition wherein extreme cold causes a creature to move slowly.

When ''chilled'', subjects movement speed is reduced to half of normal (overlapping any other movement-reducing effects).

Any effect that causes ''chilled'', when cast by a rank 2 or higher practitioner, and lands upon a creature already ''chilled'', instead causes [rooted](../conditions/rooted) (Con ends, replaced with chilled, Con ends).
